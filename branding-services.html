FAQs

Branding questions clients usually ask first

These are the common questions behind logo design, brand identity, and marketing design work before a project begins.

What is included in a branding package?

A branding package can include logo concepts, brand colors, typography direction, social media templates, campaign graphics, and usage guidance depending on the scope you choose.

How long does a branding project take?

Most branding projects take between one and three weeks depending on the number of deliverables, revision rounds, and the level of brand strategy involved.

Can you redesign an existing logo or brand identity?

Yes. We can refresh an outdated logo, improve consistency, and expand an existing brand into a stronger visual identity system.

Do you also design marketing materials and social media assets?

Yes. Branding work can be extended into branded posters, promo visuals, ad creatives, social media templates, and other campaign-ready assets.

Do you work with clients outside Cameroon?

Yes. We support branding projects for businesses in Cameroon and remote markets through structured communication and digital delivery.

Need a brand that feels ready for bigger opportunities?

We can shape a visual identity system that makes your business easier to trust, remember, and market with confidence.
