# Kumtech Gateway SEO Keyword Map

## Primary Positioning

Kumtech Gateway should rank around three layers of intent:

- Local trust: `web design Cameroon`, `digital agency Yaounde`, `branding agency Cameroon`
- National demand: `website design for businesses in Cameroon`, `SEO services Cameroon`, `Google Business profile help Cameroon`
- Remote and international intent: `remote web design agency`, `branding agency for startups`, `SEO and digital growth agency`

## Success Standard

The site is moving in the right direction when:

- More pages rank for clear service keywords
- Case studies become entry pages from search
- Blog posts bring non-brand traffic
- Organic visitors convert into calls, WhatsApp chats, and quote requests
- Traffic grows from both Cameroon and non-Cameroon markets
